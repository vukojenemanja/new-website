I was good at my job.
I was also slowly disappearing.

Years in corporate consulting. A career that made sense on paper. And then one day the feeling arrived - quietly, clearly - that my life was passing by. And I was stuck with it, for quite some time. That search became this work.

The Inner Shift Method

Coaching and energy healing
working as one.

After years of working with individuals, teams, and people in corporate environments - I developed this approach because I saw what talk alone could not do. The mind understands. The body and energy field are where the real holding is. We work at both levels in every session.

01
We find the root

Through conversation and meditative techniques, we go below the surface story to find where the pattern actually began. Not just what happened - but where it is still held inside you today.

02
We release it completely

With energy healing, we release what was found - from the emotional, physical, and energetic levels of your being. Not by force. By finally giving it the space it has always been asking for.

03
You return to yourself

The weight you carried in does not leave with you. What was trapped releases - and that energy comes back to you. People feel it immediately. Lighter. Freer. More themselves.

"I don't just help you understand what is holding you back. We actually remove it."

This is what makes this method different from regular coaching and from energy healing alone.

Coaching without energy work leaves the root untouched. Energy work without coaching can miss the meaning. Together, they create a complete process - one that works on every level of who you are.

Why this combination works

Coaching shows you the root.
Energy healing removes it.

That is why this combination works where everything else only goes halfway.

With coaching alone, you understand your patterns - but they stay in the body. With energy healing alone, you feel better - but you do not know why or how to build on it.

Together, they create a complete process. We find exactly where the weight lives. Then we release it - not just from your mind, but from your body, your energy field, every level where it has been held. What was trapped comes back to you as energy, calm, and clarity.

That is the shift people feel when they leave a session.
