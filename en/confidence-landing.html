Who made this

I was a competitive dancer who couldn't make himself dance.

I trained for years. I competed. I performed. Tango became my world. And then I would arrive at a milonga - a social tango gathering - find a seat near the wall, and just watch.

Afraid of making a mistake. Afraid of being seen. Afraid of not being enough. I didn't believe in myself.

And it wasn't only tango. That same fear lived in my career, in my relationships, in every room I walked into. The confidence I showed on the outside was not what I felt on the inside.

Finding my way back - not through willpower, but through a deeper process of learning to feel safe in my own body - changed everything. This guide is the path I walked.
